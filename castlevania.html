This show is about a vampire hunter fights to save a besieged city from an army of otherworldly beasts controlled by Dracula himself.

It follows the last surviving member of the disgraced Belmont clan, Trevor Belmont, trying to save Eastern Europe from extinction at the hands of Vlad Dracula Tepes.
